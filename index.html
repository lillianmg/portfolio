Hi! I'm Lillian

Full-Stack Web Developer

Philadelphia, PA

I'm a Full-Stack Developer and UI/UX Designer pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science with a concentration in Data Analytics. My love for tech started as the daughter of engineers, growing up surrounded by computer books and tinkering with all kinds of software and hardware. I went to school for International Business but never thought about turning my passion into a career until I decided to just go for it and go back to college. I wouldn't have it any other way, because this unconventional path gave me a rich background across banking, security systems, retail operations, over a decade of customer service, and help desk tech support. This journey gave me a rare ability to understand both the people who build technology and the people who use it every day. Bilingual and cross-cultural, I'm skilled at translating complex technical concepts into simple, clear language that resonates with engineers, stakeholders, and end users alike. I'm active in my community through volunteering and regularly attend networking events, conferences, and workshops to stay current in an ever-changing tech landscape. That rapid evolution doesn't scare me. It energizes me.
